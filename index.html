Bhai Beej Special Menu

Celebrate the bond of sibling love with our specially curated menu for Bhai Beej.

Sweet

Mohanthal

A rich and traditional Indian sweet made from gram flour (besan), ghee, and sugar, flavored with cardamom and garnished with slivers of nuts. Perfect for celebrations.

Appetizers

Fulwadi

A crispy, savory snack made from gram flour, deep-fried to a golden brown with spices like cumin, sesame seeds, and ajwain.

Papad

A thin, crispy wafer made from seasoned lentil, chickpea, or rice flour, perfect with chutneys or dips.

Raita

A refreshing yogurt-based side dish, mixed with vegetables and spices to cool the palate.

Main Course

Puri

A deep-fried, round, and fluffy bread made from wheat flour, ideal for scooping up curries.

Bataka Nu Shak

A traditional Gujarati potato curry with a flavorful blend of spices, tomatoes, and green chilies.

Vaal Nu Shak

A Gujarati dish with sprouted and cooked vaal beans, prepared with spices and a hint of jaggery for balance.

Rice

Perfectly cooked Jasmine Rice.

Gujarati Daal

A tangy, mildly sweet lentil soup made with toor dal, spices, and a hint of lemon.
